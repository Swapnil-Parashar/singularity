About Us

Welcome to Singularity Career Institute, where we nurture a passion for learning and excellence. Specializing in science and math education for grades 5 to 10, and offering comprehensive courses in physics, chemistry, mathematics, and biology for classes 11 and 12, we are dedicated to equipping students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed academically and beyond.

School Classes

At Singularity Career Institute, we offer a robust and dynamic curriculum designed to meet the educational needs of students from primary through secondary school.

For students up to Class 10, we focus on building strong foundations in Mathematics and Science. Our engaging and interactive lessons make complex concepts accessible and enjoyable, fostering a deep understanding and love for these critical subjects.

For Classes 11 and 12, we provide specialized instruction in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. Our comprehensive approach ensures that students are well-prepared for higher education and competitive exams. Both of our esteemed teachers share their expertise by teaching on alternate days, offering diverse perspectives and innovative teaching methods that enrich the learning experience.
